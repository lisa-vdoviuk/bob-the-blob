B. The "Build" Mechanic
 
E.g:(What exactly do the blobs build?)
	- (Idea) - “Name” 	
They form different shapes depending on how many people interact with them at the same time - Oleksii



Silent Mode
When quiet, blobs are sleeping or swimming slowly. They may glow slightly as if they are breathing.
Curiosity Mode
A soft voice or whisper wakes them up. They swim closer, become lighter, move cautiously.
Build Mode
A steady sound, rhythm, or multiple voices activate construction. Blobs turn green and begin to form structures.
Harmony Mode
When the sound is not just loud, but “harmonious” or rhythmic, they build the most beautiful shapes: towers, bridges, flowers, waves, spirals.
Overload Mode 
If the sound is too loud or chaotic, the blobs panic, turn red, scatter, and the structure collapses.

One voice → small simple structures, such as columns or cubes.
Two or three voices → blobs start to build bridges or arches.
Many voices → complex hive-structures appear: crystals, trees, domes, spirals.
Rhythmic sound → structure grows in time.
Harmonious sound/singing → structure becomes symmetrical and beautiful.
Chaotic sound → structure is unstable or falls apart
Blobs remember the interaction.
For example, after a good collective interaction, the structure does not disappear immediately, but remains as a glowing monument. This shows that people have “left a mark” in the world of blobs.
You can call it:
Sound Memory
The blobs remember moments of harmony and transform them into glowing structures.
This adds poetry and makes the installation deeper.
“Lisa”

This project is an immersive audiovisual installation developed in TouchDesigner, centered around a population of small, jelly-like cube creatures (“blobs”) living in an abstract virtual environment.
